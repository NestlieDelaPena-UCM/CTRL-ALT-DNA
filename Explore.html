GMO Evolution Timeline

1973

The Beginning

Herbert Boyer and Stanley Cohen create the first genetically modified organism using recombinant DNA technology.

1982

Medical Breakthrough

FDA approves the first GMO product: human insulin produced by genetically modified bacteria.

1994

First GMO Food

Flavr Savr tomato becomes the first genetically modified food approved for commercial sale.

2000

Golden Rice

Golden Rice, engineered to produce beta-carotene, is developed to combat vitamin A deficiency.

2015

CRISPR Revolution

CRISPR gene-editing technology gains widespread adoption, making genetic modification more precise.

2025

Modern Era

GMO crops grown on over 190 million hectares globally, addressing food security and climate challenges.
